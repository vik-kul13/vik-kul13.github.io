Strategic Landscape: SWOT Analysis

Strengths

  • Disruptive Innovation: Proven ability to create 5-star, first-to-market products.
  • Strong Financials: Robust sales growth and increasing profitability.
  • Diversified Portfolio: Presence across 35+ categories reduces risk.

Weaknesses

  • Cost Pressures: Exposed to rising costs and significant tariff impacts.
